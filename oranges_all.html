Agridera Fresh Market Orange Hybrids

×××××

Agridera aspires to be among the global leaders in the cultivation, production, and distribution of orange seeds. Our dedication to research and development ensures solutions for growers and consumers worldwide. We specialize in creating disease-resistant varieties tailored to diverse markets.

Presently, our efforts concentrate on integrating resistances to diseases like Citrus Greening and Citrus Canker, alongside other soil-borne pathogens, to develop superior orange varieties. Additionally, we're actively working on enhancing resistance against emerging threats like Citrus Black Spot.

Our breeding programs prioritize traits such as early maturity, varied fruit sizes, firmness, vibrant orange color, flavor, resistance to cracking, extended shelf life, and adaptability to various temperature ranges. We aim to offer a range of oranges, including traditional round varieties and elongated shapes, with a focus on quality and disease resilience.

Agridera's orange breeding programs concentrate on three primary categories: Navel Orange, Sanguineum Pernambuco, and Sanguinello Moscato.

Navel Orange Breeding Program

This program targets markets demanding high-quality oranges with easy-to-peel, seedless, and sweet characteristics. Our focus is on developing varieties with optimal taste, texture, and shelf life.

Sanguineum Pernambuco Breeding Program

Geared towards regions with tropical climates, such as Brazil and parts of Africa. We prioritize disease-resistant varieties, emphasizing resistance to citrus greening (HLB), while ensuring superior flavor and juiciness.

Sanguinello Moscato Breeding Program

Tailored for markets valuing unique citrus flavors and aesthetic appeal. We aim to create varieties with rich, aromatic taste profiles and distinctive red flesh, suitable for both fresh consumption and culinary applications..

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
